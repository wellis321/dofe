## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### If Internet Fails:
1. Stay calm - tell them "I'm experiencing a connection issue"
2. Try phone hotspot immediately
3. If that fails: "Can we reschedule? I'm having technical difficulties."
4. Follow up immediately with email explaining situation

### If You Blank on Content:
1. Take a breath
2. Look at your notes (that's why they're there!)
3. Say: "Let me clarify that point..."
4. Refer to your slides as prompts
5. Keep going - everyone blanks sometimes

### If Demo Doesn't Work:
1. Switch to screenshots immediately
2. Say: "I have prepared screenshots in case of technical issues"
3. This shows foresight and preparation!
4. Continue smoothly

### If You Run Over Time:
1. Know which sections you can condense
2. At 8-minute mark, start moving faster
3. Prioritize: Login process & top 2 problems
4. Skip or summarize less critical sections
5. End strong with summary

### If You Finish Early:
1. Don't panic - quality over quantity
2. Ask: "Would you like me to go into more detail on any section?"
3. Use backup slides if appropriate
4. Open for questions early
5. Never apologize for being concise

### If They Ask Unexpected Question:
1. It's okay to pause and think
2. "That's a great question, let me think about that for a moment"
3. Draw on your actual experience
4. If truly stumped: "I'd need to research that to give you an accurate answer, but my initial thought is..."
5. Be honest - they'll appreciate integrity
