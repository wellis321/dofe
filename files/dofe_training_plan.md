## Session Structure (10 minutes)

### 1. Introduction (1 minute)
**What you'll say:**
"Good morning! Today I'm going to show you how to log into Instagram - a social media platform where people share photos and connect with others. Even if you've never used social media before, by the end of these 10 minutes, you'll understand exactly how to access Instagram safely and what to do if something goes wrong."

**Why this works:**
- Sets clear expectations
- Reassures nervous learners
- Establishes friendly, supportive tone

### 2. What is Instagram & Why Log In? (1.5 minutes)
**Key Points to Cover:**
- Instagram is like a digital photo album you share with friends
- You need an account (like having a library card)
- Logging in proves it's really you accessing your account
- Brief mention: available on phones, tablets, computers

**Visual Aid:** Show Instagram logo and main interface screenshot

### 3. The Login Process - Step by Step (4 minutes)
**Demonstration Flow:**

**Step 1: Finding Instagram**
- Open web browser or app store
- Type "Instagram" in search
- Click the official Instagram (show the verified logo)

**Step 2: The Login Screen**
- Identify the two boxes: Username/Email and Password
- Explain what each means in simple terms
- Show where the "Log In" button is

**Step 3: Entering Your Details**
- Demonstrate typing (slowly, clearly)
- Explain password dots/asterisks are for security
- Show the "show password" eye icon option

**Step 4: Clicking Log In**
- What happens next (loading, then your feed appears)
- What success looks like

**Pro Tip:** Use annotation/pointer to highlight each element as you discuss it

### 4. Common Problems & Solutions (2.5 minutes)
**Problem 1: "Forgot Password"**
- Where to find the link
- What happens when you click it
- Checking email for reset link
- Creating a new, memorable password

**Problem 2: "Incorrect Username or Password"**
- Check caps lock is off
- Check spelling carefully
- Try typing in a notes app first to see it clearly

**Problem 3: "Suspicious Login Attempt"**
- This is Instagram protecting you
- How to verify it's really you (code to phone/email)
- Why this is a good thing

**Problem 4: "Account Disabled"**
- What this means
- How to appeal/contact support
- Prevention tips

### 5. Top Tips for Safe Login (1 minute)
**Quick Security Checklist:**
1. Never share your password with anyone
2. Use a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
3. Log out on shared devices
4. Be suspicious of emails asking for your password
5. Enable two-factor authentication (brief explanation)

### 6. Summary & Questions (30 seconds)
**Recap:**
"Today we've covered how to log into Instagram, what to expect, and how to solve the most common problems. Remember: everyone was a beginner once, and it's okay to go slowly."

**Invite questions** (acknowledge they'll ask questions after)

## Accessibility Considerations

### Visual
- High contrast slides
- Large fonts
- Describe what you're showing ("I'm now clicking the blue Login button in the bottom right")
- Provide alt text descriptions

### Auditory
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Repeat key information
- Provide written materials (slides + handout)

### Cognitive
- Simple language, no jargon
- One concept at a time
- Frequent summaries
- Visual + verbal information

### Questions to Ask Beforehand
"Do you need any specific adjustments to make this training session work for you? For example:
- Larger text
- Different color schemes
- Slower pace
- Sign language interpreter
- Written transcript"

## Questions They Might Ask You After

### About the Training
1. "How would you adapt this for different learning styles?"
   - **Your answer:** Mention visual (slides), auditory (verbal explanation), kinesthetic (hands-on practice), reading/writing (handout)

2. "What if someone still doesn't understand after your training?"
   - **Your answer:** Follow-up 1-on-1 sessions, alternative formats (video they can pause/rewind), peer learning buddies

3. "How would you measure if this training was effective?"
   - **Your answer:** 
     - Short quiz after training
     - Follow-up survey 1 week later ("Have you logged in successfully?")
     - Support ticket analysis (fewer login issues = effective training)

4. "How would you scale this to 100 staff members?"
   - **Your answer:** 
     - Recorded version + live Q&A sessions
     - Train-the-trainer approach
     - LMS with tracked completion
     - Blended learning (online + optional in-person)

### About Remote Working
5. "How do you stay productive working remotely?"
   - **Your answer:** Structure (set hours), dedicated workspace, regular breaks, clear communication with team, project management tools

6. "How do you build relationships with colleagues remotely?"
   - **Your answer:** Regular video calls, virtual coffee breaks, overcommunicate, be responsive, occasional in-person meetups

7. "What challenges do you face working remotely and how do you overcome them?"
   - **Your answer:** 
     - Challenge: Isolation → Solution: Regular team check-ins, join virtual communities
     - Challenge: Communication gaps → Solution: Overcommunicate, use video when possible
     - Challenge: Work-life balance → Solution: Set boundaries, end-of-day routine
