Understand the DofE and the role you are interviewing for

Capture the organisation’s mission, governance, and priorities plus the responsibilities you will own in the IT Knowledge & Content Coordinator position.

About The Duke of Edinburgh’s Award

The DofE has empowered young people since 1956 and now operates in over 130 countries. Their vision of “Youth Without Limits” guides inclusive programmes in Volunteering, Physical, Skills, and Expedition. They support licensed partners—from schools to youth groups to hospitals—while prioritising safeguarding, accessibility, and an impactful expansion strategy.

Current organisational priorities:
  • Modernising digital platforms like eDofE, ensuring they stay intuitive and accessible.
  • Strengthening knowledge sharing so staff can confidently deliver programmes across the UK.
  • Expanding reach, including global impact through the International Award Foundation, while keeping robust governance (Executive Leadership + Trustees).

Role Snapshot

As the IT Knowledge & Content Coordinator you will bridge IT, Learning & Development, and service delivery. You champion digital confidence by reviewing resources, designing training, and keeping documentation clear.

Core responsibilities:
  • Audit current IT/digital training options, identify gaps, and propose new resources.
  • Design and deliver formal and informal training on systems such as eDofE, Microsoft 365, and Dynamics.
  • Curate knowledge articles, ensure accessibility, and embed digital skills into organisational conversations.
  • Support IT service delivery and ticket resolution by capturing learnings and sharing knowledge.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to align training with strategic goals and system updates.

What they are looking for

Candidate traits:
  • Knowledge in creating and delivering IT knowledge documentation and related resources.
  • Strong written and verbal communication; able to translate technical concepts for non-technical stakeholders.
  • Experience designing blended and accessible digital training experiences.
  • Proactive problem-solver with excellent time management, collaboration, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Keen interest in technology adoption, driving efficiency, and promoting inclusivity.
